# em-playbook

A structured knowledge library on engineering leadership and operational strategy in fintech, covering how high-performing teams are built, how systems are operated under pressure, and how engineering organisations navigate the unique demands of regulated, high-stakes environments.

> For technical system design, architectures, and failure modes, see [fintech-systems](https://github.com/kratika-jain-em/fintech-systems).

> For topics, see [GLOSSARY](GLOSSARY.md)

---

## About This Repository

Building a fintech system is one challenge, while leading the team that builds and operates it is another. Regulated environments like financial services are at the intersection of engineering, legal, compliance, and finance, that creates organisational dynamics that most engineering management frameworks weren't designed for.

In this repository, I am trying to collect materials on how strong engineering leaders operate in these conditions.

## Topics

### Engineering Strategies
How high-performing fintech engineering teams operate their systems, incident management, zero-downtime migrations in pipelines, testing strategies, observability design for financial systems, and security engineering under PCI DSS constraints.

### Leadership Strategies
Leadership management on hiring for domain knowledge without sacrificing engineering fundamentals, managing compliance-driven roadmaps, framing technical debt to risk-averse leadership, designing sustainable on-call culture, building cross-functional alignment with compliance and finance, and running teams that operate under genuine regulatory scrutiny.
